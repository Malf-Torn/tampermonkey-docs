# Arson Gasoline Igniter

It is a straightforward, quality-of-life Tampermonkey script for Torn's Arson Crime. It adds compact 'G' (Gasoline) and 'L' (Lighter) helper buttons to the Arson crime interface, allowing you to select your required items much faster without having to move your mouse back and forth.

## Features

* **Quick Selection Buttons:** Adds handy 'G' (Gasoline) and 'L' (Windproof Lighter) buttons directly next to the crime buttons.
* **Zero Configuration:** Plug and play. The script runs automatically on the Arson crimes page, with no settings menus to configure.

## How It Works

The script seamlessly integrates into the Arson crime layout when an item is required (specifically when only one item slot is shown). 

Instead of moving your mouse to open the dropdown and then moving it again to find the specific item:
1. Click the **'G'** or **'L'** button once to automatically open the item selection popup.
2. Click the exact same button a second time to instantly select Gasoline or the Windproof Lighter.

This allows you to quickly double-click in the exact same spot to equip your desired item, making your crime-doing process significantly smoother!
